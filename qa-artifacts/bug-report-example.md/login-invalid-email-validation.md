## Bug Report 2

**Title:** Login form accepts invalid email format

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 10
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the login page
2. Enter `invalidemail` in the email field
3. Enter any password
4. Click "Login"

**Expected Result:**
The system should display a validation error for invalid email format.

**Actual Result:**
The form is submitted without validation error.

**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** High
