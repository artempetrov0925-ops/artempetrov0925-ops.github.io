## Bug Report 10

**Title:** Cart item quantity cannot be decreased using minus button

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 10
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Add any product to the cart
2. Open the cart page
3. Click the minus button next to the item quantity

**Expected Result:**
The quantity should decrease by one.

**Actual Result:**
Nothing happens when clicking the minus button.

**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** High
