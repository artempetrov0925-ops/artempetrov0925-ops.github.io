## Bug Report 7

**Title:** Empty contact form can be submitted without required field validation

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 11
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the contact page
2. Leave all fields empty
3. Click the "Send" button

**Expected Result:**
The form should prevent submission and show validation errors.

**Actual Result:**
The form is submitted without any validation messages.

**Severity:** High  
**Priority:** High
