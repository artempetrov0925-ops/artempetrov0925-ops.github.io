## Bug Report 9

**Title:** Product page takes too long to load on first visit

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 11
- Device: Desktop
- Network: Standard home internet

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the homepage
2. Click any product card
3. Measure page load time

**Expected Result:**
The product page should load within 2–3 seconds.

**Actual Result:**
The product page loads in 7–8 seconds.

**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** Medium
