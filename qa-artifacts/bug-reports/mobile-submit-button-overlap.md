## Bug Report 6

**Title:** Submit button overlaps form text on mobile resolution

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Android 13
- Device: Mobile
- Resolution: 390x844

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the contact page on mobile
2. Scroll to the contact form
3. Observe the submit button and text fields

**Expected Result:**
The button and text should be displayed correctly without overlap.

**Actual Result:**
The submit button overlaps nearby text and reduces readability.

**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** Medium
