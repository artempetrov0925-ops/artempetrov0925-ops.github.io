## Bug Report 8

**Title:** Dropdown menu does not close after selecting an option

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 11
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the filter section
2. Click the category dropdown
3. Select any option

**Expected Result:**
The dropdown should close after the option is selected.

**Actual Result:**
The dropdown remains open.

**Severity:** Low  
**Priority:** Medium
